Mutton Liver Curry

Despcription: A spicy and flavorful curry made with tender mutton liver, cooked with a blend of aromatic spices and herbs. Perfect for a hearty meal.

Ingredients:

  • Mutton liver - half kg
  • Onion - 3
  • Tomato - 1
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 spoon
  • Garlic - crushed (10 cloves)
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander
  • Chilli powder - 2 spoon
  • Pepper powder - 2 spoon
  • Turmeric powder - a pinch
  • Garam masala - half spoon
  • Salt - as required
  • Water - as required
  • Oil

Instruction:

  • Step 1: Preparation of pepper powder: Add 2 spoon of pepper and 2 - 3 drops of oil, fry it under low flame and grind them to powder
  • Step 2: Preparation of crushed fried-garlic: Add 10 cloves of garlic and 2 - 3 drops of oil and fry it under low flame and crush them
  • Step 3: On a dry kadai, add 4 tablespoon of oil and 3 finely chopped onions and saute it till it turns golden-brown color.
  • Step 4: Add 2 spoons of ginger-garlic paste, curry leaves and coriander and saute 1 minute.
  • Step 5: Now add 1 tomato and saute it well
  • Step 6: Once tomato sauteed well add half kg of mutton liver and saute for 2 minutes.
  • Step 7: Now spices like 2 spoons of chilli powder, pepper powder, half spoon of garam masala, a pinch of turmeric powder, a pinch of salt and already prepared crushed fried-garlic and mix them up well.
  • Step 8: Add required amount of water and boil it on full flame. Once the bubble pops turn the gas to medium flame, close the lid and boil for 20 minutes.
  • Step 9: Once the oil floats, uncover the lid and boil under simmer for 2 - 3 minutes.
  • Step 10: Our delicious and spiciest mutton liver curry is ready to have with idli, dosa, appam or idiyappam.
